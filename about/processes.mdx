Understanding the operating model is key. This page outlines core aspects of the system.

## Projects

Effectively freelance gigs, projects are one of three types of opportunities listed on [NEARN](https://nearn.io). Contributors fill out a form to apply, then a single winner is picked by the agency.

<Card title="What is a Project?" horizontal href="https://docs.nearn.io/contributor/project-guide" img="https://docs.nearn.io/img/sponsor/complete-payment.png" cta="Read the docs">
  NEARN helps builders get hired to complete specific tasks.
</Card>

## Treasury

MultiAgency has a Sputnik DAO account on [Trezu](https://trezu.org), which enables fast multi-chain payments from a single treasury—no switching networks or juggling wallets. Plus, it supports role-based access control and other useful settings.

<Card title="What is a Treasury?" horizontal href="https://docs.trezu.org/what-is" img="https://mintcdn.com/neardev/qOnuJ4U_--engK2H/assets/docs/landing.svg?w=2500&fit=max&auto=format&n=qOnuJ4U_--engK2H&q=85&s=d949cbf6682c1e46018816298c77afe6" cta="Read the docs">
  Trezu allows teams to securely manage crypto assets across 35\+ blockchains...
</Card>
